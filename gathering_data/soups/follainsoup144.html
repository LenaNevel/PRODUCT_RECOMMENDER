Purify your pores

For oily, acne-prone skin with stretched out pores, this mask brings everything back into balance. The complexion-clearing powers of manuka honey, rich mineral actives and purifying activated charcoal eliminate blackheads, reduce oil and wipe problems away to unearth your best skin in one step.

Show More
How to use

Apply a thick layer onto clean skin for 20-30 minutes, then rinse with cool water applied with a cloth in a circular motion. Use once or twice a week.

Hints:

Try this mask as a spot treatment for sudden, angry breakouts.

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Manuka Honey

Its antibacterial properties make it effective at fighting acne, reducing redness and healing inflamed and congested skin. It also promotes skin regeneration and reduces scarring.

Gotu Kola

Especially potent for improving elasticity, Gotu Kola is rich in amino acids, beta carotene and omega fatty acids.

Activated Bamboo Charcoal

Speckled with tiny receptor pores, it literally traps chemicals and draws out pollutants, dirt and oil from your own pores.
