Prized vintage

Vintner’s Daughter maker April Gargiulo grew up in the Napa Valley wine industry, and this serum is as rare as the finest vintage. Its 22 active botanicals are carefully selected to bring to life both efficacy and quality. Active Botanical Serum’s formula delivers the most optimal ratio of vitamins, minerals, phytonutrients, antioxidants, fatty acids, and omegas in its powerhouse formula.

Show More
How to use

Use the Vintner’s Daughter “push/press” method as part of your nightly cleansing : Rub 4-6 drops of the serum between your palms and push and press over your face, neck and décolleté until you feel it “pop” into pores.

Hints:

Inhale deeply and let the serum’s nurturing jasmine scent and healing properties envelop you.

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Grape Seed Oil

High in fatty (think linoleic) acids, grapeseed oil helps reduce the appearance of wrinkles.

Carrot Seed Oil

Derived from the wild seeds of the carrot plant, carrot seed oil is loaded with antioxidants and skin-nourishing properties.

Neroli Oil

It keeps skin smooth and glowing, and helps the epidermis maintain the right levels of oil and moisture.
