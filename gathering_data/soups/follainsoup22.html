Balancing act

Cleansing requires a combination of ingredients strong enough to remove impurities, yet gentle enough that they won’t strip or damage skin. Formulated for normal and combination skin, this cleanser nails the art of balance: wild plum extracts brighten, refresh, and protect, while calming linden flower helps keep things in check.

Show More
How to use

Squeeze a quarter-sized amount into hands and gently massage over dampened face, neck and décolleté for 30 to 60 seconds before rinsing. Avoid eye area. Use twice daily.

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Terminalia Ferdinandiana (Kakadu Plum) Extract

Kakadu Plum is the highest source of vitamin C that exists in the natural world. It breaks down melanin and helps to reduce the appearance of sun spots.

Salvia Sclarea (Clary Sage) Oil

Tones and strengthens skin, protecting it from the appearance of sagging. Helps soothe skin, and delivers powerful antioxidants to help protect skin from environmental stress.

Sambucus Nigra (Elder) Fruit Extract

Antioxidant-rich, Elderberry detoxifies the complexion quickly without stripping skin.

About The Brand

Pangea Organics is inspired by the relationship between people and the planet. They believe the earth offers everything we need to maintain health, vibrancy, and a youthful glow, so they fill their products with nutrient-dense and active ingredients sourced from all over the world. In return, they contribute to the earth’s health and vibrancy by practicing fair-trade organic sourcing.
