How to use

Apply a thick layer over face, neck and chest; rinse with warm water after 15-30 minutes. Use every day for a few days, then pare back to 1-2 per week for sustained results.

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

White Willow Bark Extract

Similar in chemical properties to aspirin (and often used as an aspirin substitute), it’s a powerful anti-inflammatory that doubles as an effective exfoliator and astringent.

Pomegranate Extract

The high antioxidant content of pomegranate extract eliminates the free radicals that damage cells and accelerate aging, and also protects the skin.

Beet Root Extract

Rich in Vitamin C and potassium, beet root extract helps to even skin tone and strengthen cells. It is loaded with antioxidants, protecting from damaging free radicals.

    • From Follain:

      Hi Kara, The ingredients you mentioned are constituents (or parts of) essential oils that are extracted to isolate desired scent profiles. There is a lot of confusion around these ingredients, so thanks for your question and concern. Although EWG lists essential oil constituents (like the ones you listed) as “allergens”, about two years ago the industry realized that this only applies to those that have gone rancid. We ONLY work with makers who disclose the sources of their ingredients so that we can ensure the highest quality, and Tata Harper certainly meets the highest standards. There is absolutely no safety issue. Please feel free to reach out with any other questions here or at help@follain.com!
