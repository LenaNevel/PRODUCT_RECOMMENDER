How to use

Smooth across lips before bed, or whenever you need a dose of soothing hydration.

Hints:

Keep this by your nightstand, but don’t be surprised if you reach for it throughout the day (It’s that good!).

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Prunus Amygdalus Dulcis (Sweet Almond) Oil

Sweet almond oil absorbs quickly into the skin, is light in texture, soothes your skin, and delivers powerful antioxidants that help protect skin against UV damage.

Blue Chamomile Extract

With antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties, Blue Chamomile is soothing to the skin and calming to the mind.
