Details
  • Helps moisturizing serums, oils, and creams absorb better (and work harder)
  • Ultra-fine hydrating mist soothes and balances skin while helping control excess oil
  • Dermatologist-tested, non-GMO, cruelty-free, and gluten-free
Your moisturizer’s best friend

Toner is one of the most misunderstood products in skincare, but we’re here to assure you: It’s not an unnecessary step. It’s actually the extra mile that makes your entire routine work better. When applied after cleansing, it creates a hydrated based to help moisturizers absorb more effectively. Plus, ours infuses skin with nourishing vitamins, minerals, and protective antioxidants. This is one product you’ll want to have on-hand all day, too: the ultra-fine mist won’t mess with your makeup, so you can use it to soothe, hydrate, and refresh your skin whenever you like. Hello, healthy glow!

Pro Tip: For an even spritz, hold the bottle about 10-12 inches from your face and press down on the pump completely to activate the micro mist.

Show More
How to use

After cleansing, spritz freely over face and neck (eyes closed!). For best results, follow with moisturizer while skin is still dewy. Use throughout the day for a quick hit of hydration.

Hints:

For an even spritz, hold the bottle about 10-12 inches from your face and press down on the pump completely to activate the micro mist.
