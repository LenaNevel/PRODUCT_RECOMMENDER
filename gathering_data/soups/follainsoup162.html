Tiny, timeless wonder

Jade rollers have been used in Chinese skincare rituals for centuries. This downsized version lets you bring the benefits with you wherever you go. The cooling effect of jade stone helps soothe and de-puff skin on contact, perfect for bringing down puffiness around the eyes.

Show More
How to use

Wash your facial roller with soap and water before each use. To start, place roller in the middle of the face and drag outwards across the cheeks. Move to the eyebrows and roll upwards over the forehead. Move below the lower lip and roll downward over the chin.

Hints:

Due to natural variations in stone, every jade roller is unique in pattern and color.
