Details
  • Gently polishes away rough patches

Polish your pucker

Kiss dry, flaky lips goodbye! This adorable lip scrub features organic sugar granules and a blend of hydrating, healing ingredients to help gently slough off dry skin and hydrate on contact, leaving you with kissably soft—and happy!—lips. Rose otto and rose geranium soothe as peppermint oil uplifts and freshens. A touch of pink from organic beet root powder, together with its super chic packaging, will have you smiling again in no time.

Show More
How to use

Gently massage a pea-sized amount onto lips; rinse. Follow with one of our lip tints for a moisturizing boost.

Hints:

Longing for a bold lip look? Use this lip polish before applying bright lip color to ensure an even, vibrant application.

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter

Moisturizes skin and helps replenish hydration lost during cleansing.

Ricinus Communis (Castor) Seed Oil

Rich in antioxidants, Castor oil leaves your skin feeling deeply moisturized, while protecting it from free radicals.
