How to use

Apply a dime size amount to damp skin and gently massage face. Rinse with warm water and pat dry.

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Aloe Vera Extract

Applied topically, Aloe Vera is a moisturizing multitasker and known to soothe the skin without the greasy feel. It contains antioxidants including, beta carotene, vitamin C and E that can help keep the skin hydrated.

Cocos Nucifera (Coconut) Oil

Encourages healthy cell turnover, nourishes and helps protects skin from damaging environmental stressors.

About The Brand

About The Brand

Herbalist, esthetician, aromatherapist, and founder of Naturopathica, Barbara Close has been a believer in holistic health and beauty since before they were cool. In 1995, she developed her product line with the mission to educate people on the connection between the two. We love that all her products are made with clean, sustainable ingredients. But we especially love that several products are certified natural and organic by Ecocert, one of the highest standards for skincare and cosmetics.
