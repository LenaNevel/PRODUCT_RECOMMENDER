Dry skin double duty

This two-in-one makeup remover and dry skin cleanser gently removes dirt, makeup and other impurities that build up on your skin throughout the day. Bursting with omegas 6 & 9 and Vitamin E, it replenishes the skin’s moisture barrier for a natural glow. The dual-textured cloth allows you to switch between a gently exfoliating muslin for thorough cleansing and an ultra-soft terry side for more sensitive areas.

Show More
How to use

Dampen the Dual-Effect Sensitive Skin Cloth with warm water and squeeze out excess. Gently massage cleanser all over dry skin, and remove using muslin-side of cloth, with gentle circular movements. Follow with final sweep over face with soft terry-side. For eyes, use a small amount of cleanser and the soft terry-side of cloth on dry lids to lift away all traces of makeup and cleanser.

Hints:

For hypersensitive skin, use only the soft terry-side of cloth.

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Camellia Seed Oil

This lightweight oil penetrates deeply to hydrate with essential fatty acids and vitamins to brighten and protect from environmental stressors. Naturally occurring squalane in the oil helps prevent premature aging; it also acts as a natural UV filter.

Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter

Moisturizes skin and helps replenish hydration lost during cleansing.

Rose Oil

Gives a boost to dull, lackluster skin, replenishing moisture and restoring radiance.

About The Brand

About The Brand

Pai founder Sarah Brown has firsthand experience with sensitive skin–and her own frustrations inspired her to launch this London-based line of products specifically formulated for sensitive skin. Pai takes its name from the Maori word for “goodness,” and every product reflects that ethos with gentle, calming and high performing ingredients.
