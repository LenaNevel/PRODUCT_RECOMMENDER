Indulge your skin every day

While we’d love to treat ourselves to a leisurely facial every single day, time isn’t always on our side. So we created a deeply hydrating, fast-absorbing moisturizer that fully pampers your skin, even if you only have a few minutes to spare. Its luxuriously smooth formula combines time-tested and innovative new ingredients; together, they take on early signs of aging while delivering a smooth, luminous complexion. The delicate scent of rosewater is the icing on the cake of this daily treat for your skin and your senses.

 

Research Results

In an independent consumer test panel of 30 women, ages 25-55, with normal, combination, and dry skin:

  • 93% agreed their skin felt moisturized
  • 90% agreed the product absorbs easily into skin
  • 86% agreed their skin felt smooth
  • 80% agreed their skin felt replenished, nourished, and comforted

 

Show More
How to use

Massage 2 to 3 pumps over clean face and neck, preferably while skin is still dewy from toner. Take a little “me time” until absorbed.

Hints:

Help your skin soak up every bit of product by gently pressing into face while applying. Given its lightweight feel and fast absorption, this is the perfect moisturizer to wear under makeup or to combine with additional treatments, such as serums or oils. Layer serums underneath and oils on top.

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Bakuchiol and Peptide Blend

Bakuchiol, a plant-derived, non-irritating alternative to Retinol, and a clinically-tested Peptide Complex work together to help improve the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

Rosehip Oil

Packed with vitamins, antioxidants and omegas, this clean skincare powerhouse helps protect, moisturize and improve skin tone and texture.

Sodium Hyaluronate

This powerful humectant penetrates deep into the skin to help attract and retain moisture, leaving skin looking smooth and supple.
