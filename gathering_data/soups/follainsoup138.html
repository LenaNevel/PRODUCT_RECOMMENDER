Not just a pretty face

This face mask isn’t just pretty to look at—it actually clarifies and cools the skin, too. The blue color comes from its star ingredient: blue tansy oil, which reduces inflammation and combats redness. Plus natural BHA and AHA gently exfoliate to reveal a smoother healthier-looking complexion.

Show More
How to use

Apply a thin layer to clean skin. Leave on for 15 to 20 minutes (stick to five minutes for sensitive skin) or until mask is completely dry and then rinse with warm water.

Hints:

For the first five days, use every other day for best results. After day five, use two to three times per week to maintain results.

Blue Tansy Flower Oil

As it soothes the skin, the aromatherapeutic benefits of this oil also calm the mind. Blue tansy balances skin, calms irritation and delivers much needed hydration to skin.
