Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Zinc

Non-nano zinc oxide provides the skin with a powerful shield against UV rays. It is a natural sunscreen, so it does not have any of the harmful additives associated with chemical sunscreens.

Evening Primrose Oil

High in omega-6 fatty acids, this oil will protect the top layer of the skin and help reduce redness while the essential oils penetrate to work on fighting what is happening just under the top layer.

Hemp Seed Oil

The fats in hemp seed oil are similar to those in our own skin, allowing this oil to be easily accepted by skin. Fatty acids help skin retain its elasticity for a youthful appearance. Vitamins A and E protect skin against free radical damage.
