A little bit softer now

We apply lip balm constantly, but our lips don’t ever seem to feel moisturized. The cycle ends here. This non-toxic lip balm is made with a nourishing trio of argan oil, shea butter, and chamomile flower oil to deeply hydrate dry, cracked lips, leaving them smooth and kissably soft. You’re going to want one of these in your car, purse, and every coat pocket.

Show More
How to use

Smooth over entire lip area to restore hydration. Apply throughout the day as needed.

Hints:

Wear it alone for a natural look, or swipe it on before lipstick for a smooth, flake-free finish.
