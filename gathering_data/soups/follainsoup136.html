Moisture on lock

Hold up — did you know removing masks with water can actually increase moisture loss? Not ideal. So Josh Rosebrook designed a unique hydrating treatment that doesn’t have to be washed off with water. You’re left with plump, youthful, dewy skin that stays as such way long after your mask moment.

How to use

Spritz face with Hydrating Accelerator, then apply an even, thick layer of Advanced Hydration Mask. Let sit for 15-25 minutes, before removing with a clean dry cloth.

Hints:

Advanced Hydration Mask can be used as a daily effective hydration support treatment.
