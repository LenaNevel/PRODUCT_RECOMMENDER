About The Brand

About The Brand

Herbalist, esthetician, aromatherapist, and founder of Naturopathica, Barbara Close has been a believer in holistic health and beauty since before they were cool. In 1995, she developed her product line with the mission to educate people on the connection between the two. We love that all her products are made with clean, sustainable ingredients. But we especially love that several products are certified natural and organic by Ecocert, one of the highest standards for skincare and cosmetics.
