Nightly nourishment

Calming and hydrating, this shea- and mango-based face balm melts on contact to moisturize and heal skin overnight. Super antioxidant organic green coffee and Vitamin E-rich prickly pear seed oil team up to calm inflammation and minimize scars, while Tamanu oil keeps acne at bay. It’s an affordably nourishing option for dry, damaged or blemish-prone skin.

Show More
How to use

Warm a pea-sized amount between fingers and apply to face, neck and chest.

Hints:

Use this balm on scars, bites, sunburned and irritated skin to calm and heal.

Show More
Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter

Moisturizes skin and helps replenish hydration lost during cleansing.

Prickly Pear Seed Oil

This fast-absorbing oil penetrates deep below the surface to restore skin’s balance and create a luminous glow. Thanks to its high fatty acid content, it reduces the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

Green Coffee Bean Oil

Rich in linoleic and oleic acids, green coffee bean oil nourishes and hydrates skin to prevent sagging, discoloration and fine lines and wrinkles. Antioxidants fight environmental aggressors which can lead to wrinkles and discoloration.
