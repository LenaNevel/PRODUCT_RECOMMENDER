Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Prunus Amygdalus Dulcis (Sweet Almond) Oil

Sweet almond oil absorbs quickly into the skin, is light in texture, soothes your skin, and delivers powerful antioxidants that help protect skin against UV damage.

Citrus Limon (Lemon) Oil

Rich in vitamin C and antioxidants, its detoxifying properties soothe and promote a healthy skin appearance.
