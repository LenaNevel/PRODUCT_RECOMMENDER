Tiny but mighty

Don’t let its size fool you: This cleansing gel offers as much grime-fighting power as any other full-size product. The secret is an ultra-concentrated formula, which allowed the 27 Rosiers team to pack more of the good stuff into a travel-friendly bottle. Just add water and watch it transform into a light foam, then wash the day away.

Show More
How to use

Apply 1 pump on your wet hands. Rub your hands together to turn the gel texture into foam. Apply and massage gently your face and neck. Rinse with water. Use morning and night.

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Moringa Oleifera Seed Extract

Known for its purifying properties, this superfruit works to protect your skin and combat environmental stressors.

Calendula Offincalis Flower Extract

Calendula is gentle enough to use on even sensitive skin, and its calming and hydrating benefits makes it ideal to help soothe skin.

Chamomilla Recutita Flower Extract

Chamomile is an antioxidant-rich ingredient that protects skin from free radical damage, provides the skin with soothing benefits, and helps improve the appearance of skin tone as it tightens the appearance of pores.

About The Brand

About The Brand

Named after the Parisian address of its co-founders, 27 Rosiers is an emerging clean beauty brand that balances the best of nature and science. Co-founders Kevin and Stephanie aspired to create products that reflected their health-conscious values, offered visible results, and catered to busy, on-the-go individuals like themselves. With 27 Rosiers, they’ve achieved just that: a non-toxic, take-anywhere routine that’s full of French flair.
