Details
  • Light daily moisture
  • Gentle and softly scented
  • Ideal for dry or sensitive skin
Soothes from head to toe

Softly scented and gentle enough for the driest complexions, this all-over complexion lotion layers skin in a veil of light lavender. It’s made for anyone and everyone–and dry, inflamed or sensitive skin (including those with nut allergies) will find solace in its moisturizing properties.

Show More
How to use

Apply to damp, warm skin in morning–or anytime (be sure to breathe in the lavender-fields fragrance). Dries to a matte finish for makeup application.

Hints:

Rescues skin that’s damaged or compromised by surgery or lasers.

Show More
Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Marigold Extract

An ancient soothing remedy, Marigold is a plant extract that is trusted to combat inflamed skin while improving the appearance of fine lines.

Lavandula Angustifolia (Lavender) Oil

An essential oil derived from the flower spikes of lavender, it possesses calming effects that are great for protecting skin from congestion.

Full Ingredient List

*Soy Oil, Marigold Extract, Hamamelis Virginiana (Witch Hazel), Lavandula Angustifolia Oil - Lavender Oil, Cera Alba (Beeswax), Vegetable Lecithin, Borax, Xanthan Gum
* Organic
