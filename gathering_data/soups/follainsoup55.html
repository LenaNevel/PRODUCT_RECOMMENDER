Details
  • Daily moisturizer in serum formulation
  • Fresh herbal scent
  • Gently tackles signs of aging
Balmy outlook

This serum-style moisturizer is ideal for skin that needs a little hydration (and some TLC) but doesn’t like to be weighed down. The freshly scented formulation absorbs quickly to a matte finish and gets to work, tackling signs of aging with potent–and safe–essential oils.

Show More
How to use

Massage a half pump onto clean, dry skin on face and neck. Works well alone or as a safe sunscreen primer.

Hints:

Apply under a richer cream for multi-layered hydration.

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Witch Hazel

As the name hints, witch hazel is nothing short of magic. It heals cuts and abrasions, clarifies skin and tightens pores, and removes excess oil.

Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Seed Oil

High in Omega-6 fatty acids and VItamin E, Sunflower Seed oil is hydrating, retains moisture and is gentle on your skin.

Myrtle Oil

Myrtle oil acts as an astringent and antiseptic, making it ideal for oily or acne prone skin.
