Pamper problems away

A powerful powder that transforms with water into a decadent mousse, this intensive mask is a tincture for complexion ills. Its soil nutrients, Activated Charcoal and antioxidant-rich spices warm on contact, melting deep into skin to magnetically pull out dirt, oil, and bacteria, calm breakouts, and unearth your most radiant skin.

How to use

Activate one tablespoon of powder with just less than an equal part water. Smooth over skin, and allow to dry fully. Rinse. For a more intense experience, try substituting water with The Honey Mud or organic yogurt or honey.

Hints:

The corrective powers of this masque also provide an effective spot treatment for breakouts.
