Soothe and smooth

This moisturizer is formulated to soothe and support even the most sensitive and reactive skin. Rich in antioxidants and omegas 3 & 6, this light silky cream leaves skin hydrated and looking beautifully healthy. Concentrated rose hip and chamomile extracts work in unison to deliver immediate calm and long-lasting relief to reactive skin, restoring sensitive complexions to their natural radiance.

Show More
How to use

Massage 1-2 pumps into clean, dry skin on face and neck. Use daily in the mornings, or whenever your skin needs a moisture top-up.

Hints:

Store in a cool dry place to keep the cream’s active ingredients most effective.

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Prunus Armeniaca (Apricot) Kernel Oil

Rich in fatty acids and Vitamins A, C and E, apricot kernel oil deeply moisturizes skin while providing it with powerful antioxidants.

Rosa Canina Fruit (Rosehip) Extract

A dense supply of essential fatty acids and vitamins A, C, and E help repair and protect the skin against environmental stressors.

Matricaria Recutita (Chamomile) Oil

This antioxidant-rich ingredient protects skin from free radical damage, provides the skin with soothing benefits, and helps improve the appearance of skin tone as it tightens the appearance of pores.

About The Brand

About The Brand

Pai founder Sarah Brown has firsthand experience with sensitive skin–and her own frustrations inspired her to launch this London-based line of products specifically formulated for sensitive skin. Pai takes its name from the Maori word for “goodness,” and every product reflects that ethos with gentle, calming and high performing ingredients.
