Details
  • Mud-like formula draws out impurities

Break your breakout cycle

A serious cleanser for oily, acne-prone skins, this potion of organic algae masquerading as mud delivers immediate relief. Amino acid-rich algae and seaweed combine with inflammation-cooling oils to decongest blemished skin without overdrying. The result is clean, tingling skin ready to work its way back to balance.

Show More
How to use

Apply to damp skin in gentle, circular motions to remove dirt, oil and makeup. Rinse and pat dry.

Hints:

Oily types will reach for this every day, while other skins will enjoy its deep-cleaning benefits as a treatment once or twice each week.

Show More
Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Kelp Extract

Antioxidants, including Vitamin A, fight free radicals and help shield collagen for damage. High in iodine and minerals, sea kelp moisturizes the skin while preventing acne breakouts and reducing inflammation. Chlorophyll helps to detoxify the skin.

Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba Seed) Oil

This gentle oil can be used anywhere on the face and body; its non-comedogenic properties won’t clog pores. Vitamin B Complex, Vitamin E and antioxidants protect skin.

Tea Tree Leaf Oil

Fights odor-causing bacteria.
