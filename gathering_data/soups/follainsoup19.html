Details

Cleans with kindness

This sponge does more than just cleanse, it pulls double-duty as a detoxifier, too. It’s a gentle, daily way to remove dead skin buildup and keep pores clean and clear. The bamboo charcoal sponge is perfect for oily, combination and problematic skin

Show More
How to use

Place your sponge under warm water until it softens and expands. Apply cleanser to the sponge and gently massage your face and neck in circular motions. Rinse thoroughly and hang to dry.

Hints:

With proper care (hang to dry – ideally outside of your moist bathroom, disinfect in hot water as needed), this sponge will baby your skin for up to three months.
