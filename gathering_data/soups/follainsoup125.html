Details

Daily detox

This gently detoxifying regenerating cleanser is results-oriented but skin-friendly. Ideal as a daily cleanser or a twice-a-week treatment, it gently clears and refines with microspherical beads–leaving skin supple and glowing while putting moisture back in balance.

Show More
How to use

Apply a generous amount to dry skin (morning and/or night), polish in circular motion, then add water. As a treatment, leave on for 5-10 minutes.

Hints:

Trouble areas? For deeper exfoliation, repeat with second application and buff in small circles to skin in need of extra decongestion.

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Pomegranate Extract

The high antioxidant content of pomegranate extract eliminates the free radicals that damage cells and accelerate aging, and also protects the skin.

Rose Clay

A milk kaolin clay that can be used by all skin types to gently detoxify, exfoliate, and increase circulation.

White Willow Bark Extract

Similar in chemical properties to aspirin (and often used as an aspirin substitute), it’s a powerful anti-inflammatory that doubles as an effective exfoliator and astringent.
