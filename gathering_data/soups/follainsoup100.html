How to use

After cleansing, apply two pumps to face, neck and decolleté daily. Follow with moisturizer of your choice. Use morning and night or anytime skin needs an intense moisture boost.

Hints:

Add this serum as an extra step to your skincare routine (after cleansing and before moisturizing) to boost hydration during cooler months.

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Sodium Hyaluronate (Hyaluronic Acid)

Works as a humectant, pulling moisture into the skin. This helps to reduce the appearance of fine lines and hydrates skin for a youthful glow. The high level of antioxidants also protect skin against environmental stress.

Red Marine Algae Extract

A powerful moisturizing agent that protects against external aggressors, while reducing the appearance of fine lines.
