Calibrate your complexion

A favorite oily and combination skin treatment, this serum brings skin back into natural balance. Absorbing quickly, it regulates sebum production to help oily skin act less oily, and combination skin self-regulate. The mildly herbal scent lends a sense of wellbeing.

Show More
How to use

Apply a half pump over the face and neck after cleansing and toning, when skin is still very damp.

Hints:

With regular use, breakout-prone skin should notice fewer breakouts and less oil throughout the day.

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Squalane Oil

Well received by skin for its lightweight texture, squalane oil absorbs quickly and deeply to hydrate skin on a deep level without leaving behind an oily residue.

Rosa Canina Fruit (Rosehip) Extract

A dense supply of essential fatty acids and vitamins A, C, and E help repair and protect the skin against environmental stressors.

Rosemary Extract

Its antibacterial powers make it an effective tool for combating acne, eczema, and excessive oiliness in skin. It also increases circulation and blood flow to the applied area.
