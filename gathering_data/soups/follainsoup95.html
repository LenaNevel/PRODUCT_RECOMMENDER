About The Brand

About The Brand

Pai founder Sarah Brown has firsthand experience with sensitive skin–and her own frustrations inspired her to launch this London-based line of products specifically formulated for sensitive skin. Pai takes its name from the Maori word for “goodness,” and every product reflects that ethos with gentle, calming and high performing ingredients.
