Brighten your outlook

For a foaming cleanser that wipes away impurities and makeup without overpowering, look no further. This bestselling formula blends the toxin-free tomato fortifier lycopene with hydrolyzed wheat protein to hydrate, tighten and brighten. The light strawberry scent brightens senses.

How to use

Massage a dab of cleanser onto wet face. Separately, apply to a wet cotton pad to remove eye makeup. Rinse and pat dry.

Hints:

Does double duty as a complexion-clearing exfoliating mask when you leave it on for 5-10 minutes.
