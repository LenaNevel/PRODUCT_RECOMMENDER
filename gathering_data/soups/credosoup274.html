        Bamboo Charcoal Purifying Mask

        This Product Is: a purifying charcoal face mask

        Good For: all skin types including dry, mature, sensitive, blemish-prone, combination and normal

        Why We Love It: LILFOX Bamboo Charcoal Purifying Mask is a powerful detoxifying and soothing weekly facial treatment, meant to cleanse and refresh the upper layer of the epidermis. This natural and luscious treatment mask works to purify and deliver minerals and nutrients to restore balance and sooth skin. When activated with your favorite facial mist (or water) this concentrated formula of mineral rich clays, activated bamboo charcoal, raw cacao, and soothing organic botanicals turns into a velvety mousse consistency. Aromas of cacao, lavender and frankincense seduce you as you take time for yourself and indulge in this vixen spa ritual.

        This item is FINAL SALE & cannot be returned

        Read More Read Less

        Activate mask by mixing one teaspoon-tablespoon of mask with a tiny amount of floral water toning mist (or water) in a bowl. 

        For skin plumping hydration, add a teaspoon or two of the Jungle Glow Rain forest Honey Enzyme Cleanser + Mask.

        Slowly trickle water or spritz toning mist over clay to create a velvety consistency. Once mixed, use face mask application brush to apply mask to cleansed skin and leave on 20-40 minutes. Never let mask become completely dry.

        Mist with floral water toning mist to extend treatment length.

        To remove, soften with a warm, wet cloth and remove mask. Rinse with cool water and spritz with floral water toning mist. Massage beauty nectar or moisturizer onto skin for one minute, then finish with more toning mist to assist with absorption of oil based face nectar.
