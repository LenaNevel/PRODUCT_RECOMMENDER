Details
  • Vitamin C, in a water soluble formula, brightens skin
  • Aloe hydrates and soothes
  • Rose hip lightens dark spots and reduces fine lines
Brighter days ahead

Think of this serum as a one-stop solution for better looking skin. The feather light, water-based formula is infused with aloe to hydrate without weighing skin down. Water-soluble, stable Vitamin C brightens and firms skin as powerhouse rose hip lightens dark spots and softens the look of fine lines and wrinkles. Edelweiss, with its anti-aging stem cell complex, infuses skin with powerful antioxidants; aspen bark delivers a natural source of salicylic acid to exfoliate and calm inflammation.

Show More
How to use

Apply 1 pump to clean, dry face, neck and chest. Use alone for lightweight hydration, or layer beneath your favorite moisturizer.

Hints:

To keep your serum as potent as possible, store it in a cool, dry place and use within 6 months of opening.

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Ascorbic Acid Polypeptide

A super-stable, water-soluble form of Vitamin C that tones, firms and brightens skin

Edelweiss Extract

An anti-aging stem cell complex with powerful antioxidant and skin-protecting properties.

Aspen Bark Extract

Natural source of salicylic acid; gently exfoliates and soothes inflammation.
