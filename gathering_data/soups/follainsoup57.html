Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Helichrysum Flower Water

Applied topically, helichrysum works to kill bacteria and fungus growth which can lead to skin disorders—including acne—and rashes. It can be added to a carrier oil of choice to prevent irritation, redness or blemishes on face and body.

Chamomile Essential Oil

Brightening chamomile helps even skin tone, diminishing discoloration, as it tightens the look of pores. Its soothing properties calm irritated, acne-prone and problematic skin; antioxidants protect against free radical damage.

Rose Hip Fruit Oil

A dense supply of essential fatty acids and vitamins A, C, and E allow it to speed up the skin’s regenerative processes and combat aging. Reduces the redness of acne, fastens the healing of sunburns and scarring, and guards against wrinkling.
