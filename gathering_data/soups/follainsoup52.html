Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Limnanthes Alba (Meadowfoam) Seed Oil

Meadowfoam oil is rich in antioxidants and is very easily absorbed by the skin, making it a great choice for hydrating the skin and scalp.

Neroli Oil

It keeps skin smooth and glowing, and helps the epidermis maintain the right levels of oil and moisture.

Calendula Offincalis Flower Extract

Calendula is gentle enough to use on even sensitive skin, and its calming and hydrating benefits makes it ideal to help soothe skin.
