Therapy in a jar

This waterless miracle beauty balm works well with all skin types but is especially wonderful for those with dry, delicate skin. The concentrated formulation cools skin while melting into a soothing, skin-balancing fluid that provides deep hydration and evokes a sense of serenity. While it’s working, the cocoon of intoxicating aromatic oils restores the spirit.

Show More
How to use

Massage a pea-sized amount into wet skin after cleansing. You can also use it as a spot treatment for problem areas, or breathe in its aromatic healing scent to steady frayed nerves.

Hints:

This potion is perfect as an eye cream or super-luxe lip balm.

Power Ingredients

Blue Tansy Flower Oil

As it soothes the skin, the aromatherapeutic benefits of this oil also calm the mind. Blue tansy balances skin, calms irritation and delivers much needed hydration to skin.

Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter

Moisturizes skin and helps replenish hydration lost during cleansing.

Helichrysum Essential Oil

Applied topically, helichrysum works to soothe, balance and improve the overall appearance of the complexion.
