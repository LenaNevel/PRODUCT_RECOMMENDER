Well-oiled machine

Forget the notion that oil-based products are bad for oil-prone skin—they’re actually key to restoring moisture and balancing your complexion. Packed with inflammation-busting Tamanu oil and skin-clearing antioxidants, this cleansing oil effortlessly melts away dirt and makeup, while softening skin and combating dullness.

How to use

Shake bottle and apply 4-6 pumps into palms. Press directly into dry facial skin, then wet hands and gently massage. Use a warm washcloth to wipe away makeup and debris.

Hints:

Pendulum Potion is designed to be the first sensorial step in your cleansing routine, prepping skin by removing dirt and makeup. Follow with [The Clean Dirt](https://follain.com/p/may-lindstrom-clean-dirt “The Clean Dirt”) or [The Honey Mud](https://follain.com/p/may-lindstrom-honey-mud) for a full spa-like experience.

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Tamanu Seed Oil

Xanthone compounds deliver anti-inflammatory healing, while anti-bacterials help protect against infection and acne breakouts. Tamanu also works to heal acne scars, evening skin tone and hydrating skin.

Cocoa Absolute Oil

Cocoa oil is wonderful at naturally healing dry, sensitive skin. It is high in fatty acids to penetrate the skin for deep hydration and also a great source of antioxidants.

Sweet Orange Oil

Antioxidants in sweet orange oil slow skin damage by protecting against environmental aggressors.
