Base layer

Formulated for any skin type, this lightweight yet highly concentrated beauty oil provides a protective first layer for skin. It’s beautiful in its simplicity: multi-purpose oils and herbal extracts come together to enhance radiance, protect from the elements and generally up skin’s smoothness before makeup goes on.

Show More
How to use

Apply 3-4 drops to clean, damp skin morning and night. Massage into skin with gentle, circular motions.

Hints:

Though the oil paves the way for any of RMS skin products, avoid the eyelids before applying RMS Beauty cream eye shadows.

Show More
Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Rosa Canina Fruit (Rosehip) Extract

A dense supply of essential fatty acids and vitamins A, C, and E help repair and protect the skin against environmental stressors.

Ashwagandha

Rejuvenating and replenishing, ashwagandha restores moisture to the skin while smoothing and firming.

Tamanu Seed Oil

Xanthone compounds deliver anti-inflammatory healing, while anti-bacterials help protect against infection and acne breakouts. Tamanu also works to heal acne scars, evening skin tone and hydrating skin.
