Baby your blemishes

Blemished skin needs love too. This spot treatment tackles acne differently, helping the skin underlying it with TLC rather than aggressive drying. The result: blemishes fade, leaving the skin behind in better condition than ever before.

Show More
How to use

Apply directly to blemishes (avoid other areas) 3-5 times each day.

Hints:

Apply as the last layer on skin to keep from spreading to other (non-blemished) areas.

Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Evening Primrose Oil

High in omega-6 fatty acids, this oil will protect the top layer of the skin and help reduce redness while the essential oils penetrate to work on fighting what is happening just under the top layer.

Citrus Limon (Lemon) Oil

Rich in vitamin C and antioxidants, its detoxifying properties soothe and promote a healthy skin appearance.

Rosmarinus Officinalis (Rosemary) Extract

High in antioxidants, rosemary combats free radical damage, prevents impurities, and helps retain moisture.
