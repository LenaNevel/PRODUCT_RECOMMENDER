Details
  • Lightweight moisture for oily skin
  • Minimizes and prevents breakouts
  • Leaves skin feeling clean and fresh
Banish blemishes

Stop breakouts in their tracks–and prevent more from gaining steam–with this non-greasy balm. A unique daily moisturizer-breakout buster hybrid product, it delivers the just-enough moisture oily skin needs while visibly reducing surface impurities and banishing breakouts.

Show More
How to use

Apply twice daily to clean skin in upward, circular motions.

Hints:

Use even when skin looks clear for best results, to protect against blemishes lurking below the surface.

Show More
Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Red Marine Algae Extract

A powerful moisturizing agent that protects against external aggressors, while reducing the appearance of fine lines.

Melaleuca Alternifolia (Tea Tree) Leaf Oil

Gently eliminates excess oil on skin’s surface without over-drying and helps diminish the appearance of blemishes and oily shine.
