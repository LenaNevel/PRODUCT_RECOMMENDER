        Juice Beauty Luminous Lip Crayon

        This Product Is: a sheer lipstick available in 8 shades

        Good For: hydrating lips with pigment

        Why We Love It: Juice Beauty Luminous Lip Crayons is a sheer lipstick with moisturizing balm finish. This long-lasting lipstick is build-able to provide stronger coverage. The non-toxic formula is made with Vitamin C & E and mango seed butter to help soften and hydrate lips. 

        Shop Luminous Lip Crayon in 9 shades:

        Carmel: pale nude

        Pebble: pink rose

        Apply directly to lips. For subtle definition outline lips first using the point of the pencil, then hold the angled edge of the pencil tip flat against lips to fill with natural-looking color. Does not require sharpening: simply swivel up to reveal more lip color.

        Tips: Provides the comfort of a moisturizing balm with the color of a lipstick in one, convenient crayon. Jumbo pencil shape allows for precise application. Start by tracing the natural line of your lip with the point of the crayon, then fill in with color.
