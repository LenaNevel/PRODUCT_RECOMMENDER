Beneath the surface

Behold: Tata’s best-selling resurfacing mask, in serum form (so you don’t have to wash it off). A combination of seven gentle acids sloughs off dead skin cells for a healthy youthful glow and plumper texture. Your makeup will go on smoother, your skin will be softer and you’ll get all the envious stares. Worth it.

How to use

After using a toner, massage one to two pumps of serum onto face and neck. Can be used alone or layered with another moisturizer for dryer skin types.

Hints:

A toner maximizes the results of this serum by allowing it to penetrate deeper into skin (so a little goes a long way).

Power Ingredients

White Willow Bark Extract
