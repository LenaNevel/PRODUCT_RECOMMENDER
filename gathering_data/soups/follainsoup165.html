Defines “beauty rest”

This eye serum combines radical antioxidants with the cooling, lifting benefits of a steel rollerball applicator. Botanicals (think: raw green coffee beans, espresso for the skin) penetrate undereye skin to restore youthful brightness, depuff, smooth fine lines, lighten circles, and make eyes look alert and awake. They’ll think you slept for hours (even if you didn’t).

Show More
How to use

Use overnight or after cleansing in the morning. Lightly roll the ball over undereye skin and pat the serum in with ring fingers. Allow it to absorb before applying makeup.

Hints:

Keep this on your desk and apply it whenever you need an instant pick-me-up throughout the day.

Show More
Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Barbary Fig Seed Oil

This readily absorbed oil penetrates deep below the surface to restore skin’s balance and create a luminous glow. It addresses signs of aging, lightening discoloration and plumping up fine lines and wrinkles, thanks to its high fatty acid content.

Green Coffee Bean Oil

Rich in linoleic and oleic acids, green coffee bean oil nourishes and hydrates skin to prevent sagging, discoloration and fine lines and wrinkles. Antioxidants fight environmental aggressors which can lead to wrinkles and discoloration.

Blue Tansy Flower Oil

As it soothes the skin, the aromatherapeutic benefits of this oil also calm the mind. Blue tansy balances skin, calms irritation and delivers much needed hydration to skin.
