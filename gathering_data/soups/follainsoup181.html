Ingredients

Power Ingredients

Zinc Oxide

A powerful barrier that protects the skin from UVA/UVB rays. Zinc oxide is a natural sunscreen, so it does not have any of the harmful additives associated with chemical sunscreens.

Green Tea Leaf Extract

Naturally rich in antioxidants, Green Tea Leaf Extract protects the skin from the damaging effects of free radicals. It helps the appearance of skin tone and combats acne.

Aloe Vera Leaf Juice

Applied topically, Aloe Vera is a moisturizing multitasker and known to soothe the skin without the greasy feel. It contains antioxidants including, beta carotene, vitamin C and E that can help keep the skin hydrated.
